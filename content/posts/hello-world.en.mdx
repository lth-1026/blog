I started a new blog. The plan is to refine the system as I write.

## Why I rebuilt it

I used to write on Notion. It was fast, but I hit some limits:

- Code highlighting and custom components were limited
- The way content surfaced in search was constrained
- I couldn't tune the design myself

const stack = {
